Things You Should Know Before You Play the Game:

  1. The game has 10 levels, and it will save automatically after completing any level.
  2. It has 2 Mode's (Normal and Dark), Dark Mode is separate from the Normal Mode and has level's as much as Normal Mode have. You can also use this Mode for playing in Night.
  3. In Dark Mode, Whenever you open Dark Mode after closing the Game, it will return to the 1st level and a invisible timmer of '1min' will start after touching the tile and you have complete the level in time. (the timmer will work only in 1st level).
    • And, when you select Normal Mode while playing Dark Mode, the timmer will only start at the level where you have left in Dark Mode.
    • And, After completing the level's with timmer, In rest of level's the timmer will be disabled.
  4. Whenever you leave the Game or level. After opening it, The music will be Automatically turned On. You can control it Manually (After opening the level only..).
  5. If you click the Main Menu button while playing any level, the game will restart from level 1(Even in Dark Mode).
  6. If you click the Retry button, the tile positions of tile's will change.
  7. After completing all Levels, It will Automatically get start from Level 1 (Even in Dark Mode).
