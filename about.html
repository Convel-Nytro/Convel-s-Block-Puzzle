Things You Should Know Before You Play the Game:

  1. The game has 10 levels, and it will save automatically after completing any level.
  2. The game has two modes: Normal and Dark. Dark Mode has the same levels and is good for playing at night. It's separate from Normal Mode.
  3. In Dark Mode, when you open it after closing the game, it starts from Level 1. A hidden 1-minute timer starts when you touch a tile, and you must finish the level in time.(Timer works only for Level 1).
    • If you switch to Normal Mode while playing Dark Mode, the timer will only start at the level you last played in Dark Mode.
    • After you finish the levels with the timer, the timer will be off for the rest of the levels.
  4. When you open the game again, music will turn on automatically. You can turn it off or on manually after the level starts.
  5. If you click the Main Menu button while playing any level, the game will restart from level 1(Even in Dark Mode).
  6. If you click the Retry button, the tile positions of tile's will change.
  7. After completing all Levels, It will Automatically get start from Level 1 (Even in Dark Mode).
